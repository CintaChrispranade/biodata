Meet The Founder

Cinta Chrispranade

Hello! I'm Cinta, the heart and soul behind Caccoffee & Bakery. My journey began in a small kitchen where I discovered my passion for creating desserts that not only taste divine but also tell a story. Combined with my love for specialty coffee, I dreamed of creating a space where these two worlds beautifully collide.

Each recipe in our menu is crafted with love, using only the finest ingredients. From our signature croissants to our carefully sourced coffee beans, every element is chosen to create an unforgettable experience for your senses.

What began as a home bakery in 2018 has blossomed into Caccoffee & Bakery, a beloved destination for dessert and coffee lovers. Our first tiny kitchen could barely fit all our baking equipment, but it was filled with big dreams.

This website was created to share our passion with more people, offering our signature creations to be enjoyed at home, at work, or wherever life takes you.
